OS for Raspberry Pi

Posted on Sat 20 January 2018 in Raspberry Pi • 1 min read

Collection of different OS Distributions for the SBC Raspberry Pi

  • motionEyeOS - a Linux distribution that turns your single board computer into a video surveillance system.

  • Diet Pi - DietPi is a extremely lightweight Debian Jessie OS. With images starting at 400MB, thats 3x lighter than 'Raspbian Lite'.

  • OpenELEC - is a small Linux based Just Enough Operating System (JeOS) built from scratch as a platform to turn your computer into a Kodi media center.

  • Ubuntu MATE - for the Raspberry Pi 2 and Raspberry Pi 3

  • RetroPie - RetroPie allows you to turn your Raspberry Pi or PC into a retro-gaming machine.

  • PiPlay - The pre built Raspberry Pi OS made for gaming and emulation.

  • Recalbox - offers a wide selection of consoles and game systems. From the very first arcade systems to the NES, the MEGADRIVE and even 32-bit platforms, such as the Playstation.

  • Pi MusicBox - standalone streaming music player for Spotify, Google Music, SoundCloud, Webradio, Podcasts and other music from the cloud.

  • Volumio - Volumio is meant to be an headless dap (digital audio player), just connect it to your home stereo system or your DAC.

  • SUSE - Linux Enterprise Server on the Raspberry Pi

  • NOOBS - Beginners should start with NOOBS

  • Minibian-wifi - MINIBIAN is a minimal Raspbian-based Linux image for Raspberry Pi that fits on a 1GB SD card.
